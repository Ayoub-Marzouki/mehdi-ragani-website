Biography


Born in Fes, Morocco, Mehdi Ragani is a distinguished self-taught artist whose creative journey spans several years. Initially focused on drawing, Mehdi’s passion for art has evolved significantly, leading him to explore the rich textures and expressive potential of painting. Specializing in expressionism, his artistic evolution is marked by a deep commitment to capturing and conveying profound emotions and complex ideas through his work.

Mehdi's art reflects his conviction that creativity holds the power to transform the world into a more beautiful and harmonious place. His paintings, rooted in the expressionist tradition, are imbued with the essence of Moroccan culture, rich with the vibrant colors and intricate details that characterize his homeland. Through his art, Mehdi aims to offer viewers a window into Morocco’s heritage, celebrating its antique spirit and timeless beauty.

With each piece, Mehdi invites audiences from around the globe to engage with the cultural richness and historical depth of Moroccan art, fostering a greater appreciation for the art and traditions of his homeland. His expressionist approach allows him to delve deeply into the emotional and symbolic layers of his subjects, creating works that resonate on both a personal and universal level.
